N - n

nikaboan   動 V. lost it…some place; 遺失在...地方了. do jino na nikaboan no nizpi mo? Where did you lose your money? 你的錢在哪裡掉了?

niomagza   動 V. having eaten the afternoon meal; 吃過午後飯. no teyka a nikinkoman a niomagza am miwalam. After eating the afternoon meal, one must rest. 吃過午飯後要休息。

niomalinged   動 V. become shorter; 變短了. ya na niomalinged o kekjit ko. My dress seems to have bcome shorter. 我的裙子變短了。

nimamong   動 V. caught fish; 捕釣的魚. ya apira o mo nimamong? How many fish did you catch? 你釣到幾條魚?

nimianak   動 V. gave birth; 已生孩子. ya na nimianak o kois namen. Our pigs just gave birth. 我們的豬生產了。

no kakanakan   名 N. childhood; 小時候. macikeykeyray ko do kararay ko no kakanakan ko pa. When I was young, I often went to sleep over at my friend’s house. 我小時候常去同伴的家裡睡覺。

niangayan   動 V. destination, having been to; 去處,去過. do jino o mo niangayan? Where did you go? 你去哪裡了?

nimangay   動 V. went, already been to; 已去,去過. ya nimangay jino si ama? Where did Dad go? 爸爸到哪裡去了?

nimaap   動 V. took; 拿了. sino ya nimaap so vakong ko? Who took my book? 誰拿走了我的書?

nimangap   動 V. took; 拿了. vonongen da no nimangap so ora. They were split evenly among the people who came to dig up the taro. 由參與過挖芋頭的人一起來均分。

niapoan   名 N. father and mother, parents; 父母，雙親. ji na pasovayi no niapoan na. His parents were unable to argue with him. 他的父母親拗不過他。

nimapo   動 V. from a certain place; 從..地方來. ka nimapo do jino? Where did you come from? 你從哪裡來?

nokaminasaraw   名 N. the day before yesterday; 前天. nokaminasaraw am, araraw no nikatazak ko a tao. My birthday was the day before yesterday. 前天是我的生日。
