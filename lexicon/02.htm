I - i

ikabo   動 V. there is no reason; 沒有的原因. ikong na ikabo no nizpi mo? How can you have no money? 你怎麼會沒有錢?

ikakey   動 V. like, like to eat; 喜歡,愛吃. ikong mo ikakey? What do you like to eat? 你喜歡吃什麼?

ikapaw   動 V. miss very much; 很想念. ko ikapaw imo. I miss you. 我想念你。

inapo   名 N. ancestor, of three generations or longer, a term of reverence; 祖先，已故的三等尊親或以上的尊親之稱. inapo namen si apen Mitozid. Si Mitozid is our ancestor. si Mitozid是我們的祖先。

ikarilaw   動 V. to pity, have compassion for, to honor, to love; 憐惜，很同情，要孝順，疼愛. ko ikarilaw si kaminan. I have compassion for my aunt. 我很同情阿姨。

ikasi   動 V. to have much pity, much compassion for, greatly love; 很同情，很憐憫，憐愛. apiapia rana o kapamalit nia so nizpi, a ipanidong so ikasi a tao. You might as well sell it for cash to help those who need help. 不如乾脆換錢來幫助需要幫助的人。
