T - t

toda apaw   動 V. to become empty and cold (to become lighter); 變得冷清(變輕了). ta toda apaw do vahay ta, si ciangay da rana nira maran mo. After your uncle and his group left, our house become very empty. 你叔叔他們走了以後，我們家變的冷冷清清了。

teykarilaw   動 V. to dearly pity, love, or have compassion for; 非常同情，非常可憐，非常疼愛. teykarilaw da o ananak a mehakay a anak da. They dearly loved their youngest son. 他們非常的疼愛他們的小兒子。

teymakarilaw   動 V. most pitiful; 最可憐. teymakarilaw o nimasbang a kanakan. Orphans are the most pitiful. 孤兒最可憐了。
