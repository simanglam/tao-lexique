K - k

kadadayan   動 V. after several…; 經過了幾.. kadadayan na so awawan am. After several years. 過了幾年以後。

kaman   名 N. each other’s father; 各自的父親. manga kakma so kaman nio. Just like your fathers, go to hell!(a curse). 如同你們的父親一樣，都去死吧(罵語)。

kamamaogan   名 N. the process of making, what happens in the process; 製造過程，經過情形. ori o kamamaogan no mivazay ya. 這就是舉行落成禮的過程。

kakanakan   名 N. a group of children, children; 一群小孩子,孩子們. ya teyrahet o iyangay da no kakanakan Jiratay. The children of Jiratay are very naughty. 漁人村的小孩子最頑皮了。

kanakan   名 N. little children; 小孩子. ya maganam o kanakan. The little children are all dancing. 小孩子在跳舞。

kanitoan   名 N. graveyard, grave for the elderly (those who are buried); 墳場，墓地,公墓，成年者的墓地(土葬者). kanitoan do Jiratay. The graveyard at Jiratay. 漁人村的墳場。

kapianoanood   名 N. singing; 唱歌，對歌唱有興趣，喜歡啍啍唱唱. mo ikakza o mo kapianoanood? Do you like singing? 你喜歡唱歌嗎?

kangay   動 V. then go to; 然後去. koman ka pa a kangay mo rana. Leave after you have eaten. 吃了飯再走吧。

名 N. going; 去的. na ikakza o na kangay do gako. He likes going to school. 他喜歡上學。

that is how it is, always like that; 都是這樣，總是如此. na kangay saon ori no ya malahet o kakawan am. That is how it is whever the wheater is bad. 每當天氣不好，就會這樣。

karawan   名 N. world; 世上,世界. mapintek do karawan. A long life. 很長命。

karoan   名 N. other, part, extra, where there are extra; 其餘的，部分的，多餘的，..多的地方. karoan namen am, ya nikoman so wakay. A portion of us ate sweet potato. 我們有一部份人吃地瓜。

kasian   動 V. used to reward; 慰勞用. o ya am, ko kasian nimo a ya nimazikna. Thank you for your hard work, this is your reward. 辛苦了,這是為慰勞你的。
